    ILRI uses CKAN for making available its data. We have created a series of extensions to connect CKAN directly to MySQL databases and convert research data into different formats.

    GIS tools

    ILRI manages it GIS data using a combination of GeoPortal, GeoServer and PostGIS. These combination help us store, curate and make available GIS data using interoperable web services like WFS and WCS.
